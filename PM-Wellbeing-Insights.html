The Key Drivers

Workload & Deadlines

  • 85% of project managers handle several projects at once. [3, 4, 7]
  • 54% of all employees say workload is their top cause of stress. [8]
  • People are 70% more likely to burn out from unreasonable deadlines. [9]

The Resource & Leadership Gap

  • 44% of project managers say a lack of resources is a top challenge. [3, 4]
  • Perception Gap: 43% of project managers feel teams are understaffed, but only 20% of senior leaders agree. [3]
  • A manager's actions account for 70% of their team's engagement. [5]

The Path to Resilience: Strong Foundations

The Success Benefit

  • Companies with mature project management practices have a 92% success rate. [4]
  • These companies also waste 28 times less money. [4]

The Wellbeing Benefit

  • After a successful project, 54% of staff feel less stressed. [3]
  • 49% feel more in control at work. [3]

The Power of a Safe Environment

Staff who feel psychologically safe are far less likely to feel stressed (27% vs. 61%) or look for a new job (19% vs. 41%). [10]
